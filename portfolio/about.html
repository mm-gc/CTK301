about

MMGC is a Chicago-based digital artist, graphic designer, digital media consultant, college student, and lover of weird organisms. Like moray eels, or Halomonas titanicae (a bacteria whose favorite food is the rusting Titanic).

Having been interested in the digital arts from a very young age, he worked hard in middle school and highschool to master a wide variety of software, most significantly programs from the Adobe Creative Cloud suite.

In college, he is constantly striving to build upon the strong foundations of his digital media skills, and gain invaluable knowledge that will serve him in his future career in video game character design and asset creation.
